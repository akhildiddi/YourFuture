Industrial Training Institute (ITI)

ITI stands for Industrial Training Institute, which is a technical education and vocational training institute in India. ITIs offer courses in various trades such as electrical, mechanical, civil, computer science, and more, aimed at providing practical and theoretical training to students to make them skilled and employable in various industries.
